Cyberpunk 2077:

is a new game from the Polish game developer "CD Projekt Red".
The game was released in 10/12/2020, and after 8 years of waiting and anticipation for the game to release, although fans seem to be disappointed...
The main reason of fan's disappointment is the major bugs in the previews generation consoles, the PS4 and the XBOX One.
Currently the company assesses the damage caused to her by the miscalculation of their game's instability and promises to release two massive patches
on January and February that are suppose to fix most if not all of the major issues with the game's performance.
But as of now only time will tell if CD Projekt Red have what it takes to turn this around for the better or not.
